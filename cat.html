Cat in Digital Era

Acrylic on Canvas

As a participant in the UoB painting contest, which is a free-for-all topic, what I chose to bring into the contest is the breath of the modern world.

Scrolling through social media, I found out that a ton of videos and images of domestic animals appear in my feeds. As always, they are those who can potentially influence people's mood to become more cheerful.

As a result, I painted an adorable cat, which is popular entertainment in our digital age and has the power to draw attention.
